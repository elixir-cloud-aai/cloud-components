# Contact Us

The project is maintained by [**ELIXIR Cloud & AAI**](https://elixir-cloud.dcc.sib.swiss/), a Driver Project of the [Global Alliance for Genomics and Health (GA4GH)](https://www.ga4gh.org/), under the umbrella of the ELIXIR Compute Platform.

To get in touch with us, please use one of the following routes:

### Bug Reports and Feature Requests

For filing bug reports, feature requests, or other code-related issues, please use the project's [issue tracker](https://github.com/elixir-cloud-aai/cloud-components/issues).

### Private and Personal Issues

For private or personal issues, more involved communication, or if you would like to join our team as a regular contributor, you can either:

- Join our [chat board](https://join.slack.com/t/elixir-cloud/shared_invite/zt-1uvebyx1e-NcC0Hof2guT9df~haBdQaw)
